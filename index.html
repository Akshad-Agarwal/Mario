Instruction









Mario Game
1. Mario earns points for most actions in the game

2. Breaking bricks will give 50 points per block

3. Each coin collected will give 200 points.

4. Different monsters give different point values when killed

5. At level end, Mario performs a jump on a flagpole. The closer he is to the flag, the more points he gets (max 5000)

6. At level end, all-time remaining is converted into points
